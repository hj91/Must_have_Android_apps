Amateur radio and weather satellite tracker and passes predictor for Android

<b>Main features:</b><ul>
<li>Calculating satellite passes for up to one week (168 hours)</li>
<li>Calculating passes for the current or manually entered location</li>
<li>Showing the list of currently active and upcoming satellite passes</li>
<li>Showing the active pass progress, polar trajectory and transceivers info</li>
<li>Showing the satellite positional data, footprint and ground track on a map</li>
<li>Offline first: passes predictions are made offline. Weekly updates of TLEs and transceivers DB are recommended.</li></ul>
